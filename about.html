About Our Job Portal

We provide a platform that connects job seekers with recruiters. Our mission is to make job hunting and hiring easier, smarter, and more efficient. Whether you're looking for a full-time position, a part-time gig, or an internship, our portal helps you find it easily by location and job type.

This platform is built entirely with frontend technologies — no backend involved — making it beginner-friendly and interactive.
